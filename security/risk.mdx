# Risk Disclosure

Investing in RondSync involves various risks. Users should fully understand
these risks before allocating capital.

---

## Overview

RondSync is a yield infrastructure that deploys capital across structured strategies.
While the system is designed with risk management in mind, **loss of capital is possible**.

---

## Market Risk

Returns depend on market conditions and may fluctuate.

- Volatility in crypto and financial markets can impact performance  
- Sudden price movements may affect strategy outcomes  
- Yield is not fixed and may vary over time  

---

## Strategy Risk

Performance depends on execution strategies.

- Strategies may underperform expectations  
- Market conditions may reduce profitability  
- Strategy changes may impact returns  

---

## Liquidity Risk

Access to funds may not always be immediate.

- Certain Vaults may impose lock-up periods  
- Early redemption may incur penalties  
- Liquidity conditions in underlying markets may affect withdrawal timing  

---

## Smart Contract Risk

On-chain interactions introduce technical risks.

- Bugs or vulnerabilities may exist in smart contracts  
- Exploits or attacks could result in loss of funds  
- Users interact with contracts at their own risk  

---

## Operational Risk

The system relies on infrastructure and execution processes.

- System failures or downtime may occur  
- Transaction execution delays may affect outcomes  
- Human or system errors may impact operations  

---

## Counterparty Risk

Capital may be deployed across external venues.

- Centralized or decentralized platforms may fail  
- Third-party services may introduce additional risk  
- Funds may be exposed to custodial environments  

---

## Regulatory Risk

The regulatory environment may change.

- New regulations may affect platform operation  
- Access to certain services may be restricted  
- Users are responsible for compliance in their jurisdiction  

---

## Security Risk

While security measures are implemented, risks remain.

- Unauthorized access attempts may occur  
- Wallet security is the user’s responsibility  
- Phishing or social engineering attacks may result in loss  

---

## No Guarantee

RondSync does not guarantee:

- Profitability  
- Stable returns  
- Capital preservation  

All investments are made at the user's own risk.

---

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for:

- Understanding all associated risks  
- Managing their own wallet security  
- Making informed investment decisions  

---

## Final Note

RondSync is designed to provide transparent and structured access to yield strategies.  
However, **risk cannot be eliminated, only managed**.
