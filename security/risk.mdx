## Overview

RondoSync provides access to structured strategies with **Share**-based Vaults and **profit** / loss exposure.
While designed with risk management in mind, **loss of capital is possible**.

Risk cannot be eliminated and may vary depending on market conditions,
strategy performance, and system factors.

---

## Market Risk

Market conditions directly impact performance.

- Price volatility in crypto and financial markets  
- Sudden liquidity changes  
- Macroeconomic and market-wide events  

Returns may fluctuate significantly and are not stable.

---

## Strategy Risk

Each Vault is based on a specific strategy.

- Strategies may underperform  
- Execution inefficiencies may reduce returns  
- Strategy assumptions may fail under certain conditions  

Strategy performance is not guaranteed.

---

## Liquidity Risk

Access to capital may be limited.

- Lock-up periods may restrict **redeem**  
- **Redeeming** early may incur penalties  
- Underlying markets may have limited liquidity  

Liquidity constraints may delay or reduce access to funds.

---

## Smart Contract Risk

On-chain interactions introduce technical risks.

- Smart contract vulnerabilities  
- Exploits or unexpected behavior  
- Integration risks with external contracts  

Users interact with smart contracts at their own risk.

---

## Counterparty Risk

Capital may be deployed across external platforms.

- Centralized exchanges or service providers may fail  
- Custodial risks may apply  
- Third-party dependencies introduce additional exposure  

Failure of counterparties may result in partial or total loss.

---

## Operational Risk

System and operational processes may fail.

- Infrastructure outages  
- Transaction execution delays  
- Human or system errors  

These risks may impact performance or fund access.

---

## Regulatory Risk

Regulatory environments may change.

- New laws may affect platform operations  
- Restrictions on access or usage may occur  
- Users must comply with local regulations  

Regulatory changes may impact availability or functionality.

---

## Security Risk

Security threats may affect users and the system.

- Unauthorized access attempts  
- Phishing or social engineering attacks  
- Wallet compromise  

Users are responsible for securing their own wallets.

---

## Redeem & withdraw risk

Capital exit is subject to conditions.

- **Redeem** settlement timing may vary  
- **Withdraw** processing delays may occur  
- Market conditions may affect final value  

Access to funds is not always immediate.

---

## No Guarantee

RondoSync does not guarantee:

- Profitability  
- Stable returns  
- Capital preservation  

All investments are made at the user’s own risk.

---

## User Responsibility

Users are responsible for:

- Understanding all associated risks  
- Making informed investment decisions  
- Managing their own wallet security  

---

## Final Note

RondoSync is designed to provide structured access to strategies
with transparency and control.

However, **risk is inherent in all financial activity and cannot be eliminated**.
