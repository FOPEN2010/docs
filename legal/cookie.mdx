## Overview

RondoSync may use cookies and similar technologies in connection with the platform.

Cookies are small data files stored on a user’s device when visiting a website or using a web-based service.

Cookies may be used to:

- Enable platform functionality
- Support authentication or session management
- Improve user experience
- Support security and fraud prevention
- Understand platform usage
- Analyze performance
- Maintain or improve the Services

Use of cookies may change over time as the platform, service providers, analytics tools, or security systems evolve.

---

## What Are Cookies

Cookies are small files that may be stored on a device, browser, or application environment.

They may help the platform:

- Remember session information
- Maintain functionality
- Improve navigation
- Analyze usage patterns
- Detect abnormal or suspicious behavior
- Support security controls

Cookies may be set by RondoSync or by third-party service providers that support the Services.

## Types of Cookies RondoSync May Use

RondoSync may use different categories of cookies or similar technologies.

### Essential Cookies

Essential cookies are necessary for the operation of the Services.

They may support:

- Basic platform functionality

- Session management

- Network or service reliability

### Functional Cookies

- General usage trends
