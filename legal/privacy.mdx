## Overview

RondoSync may collect, use, and process certain information when users access or use the platform.

This information may be used to operate the Services, support Vault participation, process platform activity, maintain internal accounting records, improve functionality, detect abuse, support security, and comply with applicable legal or regulatory obligations.

RondoSync does not sell personal data.

---

## Information RondoSync May Collect

RondoSync may collect several categories of information.

### Wallet Information

This may include:

- Wallet addresses used to access the platform
- Connected wallet information
- Wallet-related activity
- Wallet ownership or control information, where verification is required

Wallet addresses may be considered personal data under certain laws.

---

### Transaction Data

Transaction-related data may include:

- Blockchain transaction history related to platform activity
- Deposits
- Redeem activity
- Withdraw activity
- Vault participation records
- Smart contract interactions
- Network, asset, amount, and transaction status data
- Transaction hashes and confirmation data

Blockchain transaction data may be publicly visible and permanent.

---

### Platform Usage Data

RondoSync may collect information about how users interact with the platform.

This may include:

- Vault selection
- Page interactions
- Feature usage
- Activity logs
- Session data
- Device information
- Browser information
- IP address
- General location data
- Error logs or diagnostic information

This information may be used to operate, secure, improve, and troubleshoot the platform.

---

### Platform Accounting Data

RondoSync may process information related to platform balances and Vault participation.

This may include:

- **Shares**
- **Share Price** records
- Vault position records
- Rewards records
- **USD Balance** records
- Redeemed amounts
- Referral rewards
- Ambassador rewards
- Withdraw request status
- Internal accounting records

These records may be used to operate the platform, process user activity, and maintain system integrity.

---

### Security and Compliance Data

RondoSync may collect or process information for security, fraud prevention, compliance, and AML-related purposes.

This may include:

- Risk scores
- Wallet screening results
- Transaction monitoring results
- Suspicious activity indicators
- Compliance review records
- KYC or verification information, where requested
- Communications with the user
- Information from third-party service providers
- Data required to investigate fraud, abuse, sanctions exposure, or prohibited activity

Compliance-related data may be used to determine whether access, Redeem, or Withdraw should be delayed, restricted, or rejected.

---

### Cookies and Tracking Technologies

RondoSync may use cookies or similar technologies.

These may be used for:

- Essential platform functionality
- Session management
- Security and fraud prevention
- User experience improvements
- Analytics and performance measurement

For more information, users should review the Cookie Policy.

---

## How Information May Be Used

RondoSync may use collected information to:

- Provide and maintain the Services
- Enable wallet-based access
- Process platform activity
- Manage Vault participation
- Maintain internal accounting records
- Process Rewards, Redeem, and Withdraw activity
- Verify transaction status
- Detect, prevent, and investigate fraud or abuse
- Monitor suspicious activity
- Conduct compliance or security reviews
- Enforce Terms of Use and platform policies
- Improve platform functionality and user experience
- Troubleshoot technical issues
- Communicate with users where applicable
- Comply with legal or regulatory obligations

## Blockchain Data

Due to the nature of blockchain technology:

- Transactions may be publicly visible
- Blockchain records may be permanent
- Blockchain data may not be deleted or altered
- Wallet activity may be traceable
- RondoSync does not control public blockchain records

Users should understand that blockchain transparency may create privacy risks.

## Data Retention

RondoSync may retain information for as long as necessary to:

- Provide the Services
- Maintain platform records
- Process transactions and balances
- Resolve disputes
- Enforce agreements
- Support security and fraud prevention
- Comply with legal or regulatory obligations

## Security

RondoSync implements reasonable security measures designed to protect information.

However, no system is completely secure.

RondoSync does not guarantee absolute protection against:

- Unauthorized access
- Data loss
- Misuse
- Infrastructure compromise
- Third-party failures
- Security incidents

Users are responsible for securing their own wallets, devices, private keys, seed phrases, and transaction approvals.

## User Rights

Depending on the user’s jurisdiction, users may have certain privacy rights.

These may include the right to:

- Request access to personal data
- Request correction of inaccurate data
- Request deletion of certain off-chain personal data
- Object to or restrict certain processing
- Request information about data usage

These rights may be limited by legal obligations, security requirements, platform recordkeeping, fraud prevention, or the immutable nature of blockchain data.

User rights generally do not apply to public blockchain data that RondoSync cannot delete or alter.
