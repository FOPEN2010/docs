# KYC / AML Policy (Summary)

This page summarizes RondSync’s approach to compliance and risk control.

---

## Key Points

- KYC is not mandatory by default  
- RondSync may request verification when necessary  
- Compliance checks may be triggered by risk factors  

---

## Monitoring

The platform may implement:

- Transaction monitoring  
- Wallet activity analysis  
- Risk scoring mechanisms  

---

## Restrictions

Users must not engage in:

- Money laundering  
- Fraud or illegal activity  
- Sanctions evasion  

---

## Enforcement

RondSync may:

- Restrict access  
- Suspend withdrawals  
- Terminate accounts
