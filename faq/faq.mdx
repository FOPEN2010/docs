## General

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is RondoSync?">
    RondoSync is infrastructure that lets users **deposit** into Vaults,
    hold **Shares**, and participate in structured strategies.

    Users earn **profit** (the app may show **Rewards**) and manage capital
    through a transparent system.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Do I need an account?">
    No. RondoSync uses wallet-based authentication.

    - Connect your wallet
    - No email or password required
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What assets are supported?">
    Currently, RondoSync supports stablecoin-based deposits (e.g. USDT).

    Supported assets may expand over time.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Redeem & Withdraw

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is the difference between redeem and withdraw?">
    - **Redeem** → converts Vault **Shares** / position into USD balance on the platform
    - **Withdraw** → sends USD balance from the platform to your wallet

    They are separate processes.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I withdraw anytime?">
    You can withdraw available USD balance at any time.

    However:

    - Processing time may vary
    - Validation and approval may be required
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I redeem anytime?">
    Depends on the Vault.

    - Some Vaults have lock-up periods
    - **Redeeming** early may include penalties
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How long does withdraw take?">
    Processing time depends on:

    - Network conditions
    - Internal validation processes
    - Security checks

    Delays may occur during peak periods.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Security

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is RondoSync safe?">
    RondoSync is designed with multiple security layers:

    - Policy-based transaction controls
    - Secure wallet infrastructure
    - Monitoring and anomaly detection

    However, no system is completely risk-free.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Who controls the funds?">
    Funds are managed through a structured system:

    - User deposits are routed via smart contracts
    - Operational controls govern execution
    - Policies restrict unauthorized movement
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What happens if something goes wrong?">
    Risks exist, including:

    - Smart contract issues
    - Counterparty failure
    - Market-related losses

    Users should understand all risks before participating.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Technical

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to sign transactions?">
    Yes.

    - Deposits require wallet signatures
    - **Withdraw** does not require user signatures
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Are transactions on-chain?">
    Deposits and certain interactions are on-chain.

    Other processes may involve off-chain systems
    for execution and settlement.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
