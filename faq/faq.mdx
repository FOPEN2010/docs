# FAQ

This section addresses common questions about RondSync,
including usage, risks, and system behavior.

---

## General

### What is RondSync?

RondSync is a yield infrastructure that allows users to allocate capital
into structured strategies through Vaults.

Users can participate in yield generation and manage their capital
through a transparent system.

---

### Do I need an account?

No. RondSync uses wallet-based authentication.

- Connect your wallet  
- No email or password required  

---

### What assets are supported?

Currently, RondSync supports stablecoin-based deposits (e.g. USDT).

Supported assets may expand over time.

---

## Investment

### Are returns guaranteed?

No.

- Returns are not guaranteed  
- Performance depends on market conditions and strategy execution  

All investments carry risk.

---

### How is yield generated?

Yield is generated through structured strategies.

- Market opportunities  
- Liquidity provisioning  
- Strategy execution  

Returns vary depending on performance.

---

### Can I lose money?

Yes.

- Negative performance is possible  
- Market conditions may impact results  
- Strategy risks apply  

Users should invest responsibly.

---

### How do I choose a Vault?

Consider the following:

- Risk tolerance  
- Lock-up conditions  
- Strategy characteristics  

Each Vault has different parameters.

---

## Redemption & Withdrawal

### What is the difference between redemption and withdrawal?

- Redemption → converts Vault position into USD balance  
- Withdrawal → transfers USD balance to your wallet  

They are separate processes.

---

### Can I withdraw anytime?

You can withdraw available USD balance at any time.

However:

- Processing time may vary  
- Validation and approval may be required  

---

### Can I redeem anytime?

Depends on the Vault.

- Some Vaults have lock-up periods  
- Early redemption may include penalties  

---

### How long does withdrawal take?

Processing time depends on:

- Network conditions  
- Internal validation processes  
- Security checks  

Delays may occur during peak periods.

---

## Fees

### Are there any fees?

Yes, fees may include:

- Performance fees  
- Withdrawal-related costs  
- Network transaction fees  

Fee structures may vary by Vault.

---

## Security

### Is RondSync safe?

RondSync is designed with multiple security layers:

- Policy-based transaction controls  
- Secure wallet infrastructure  
- Monitoring and anomaly detection  

However, no system is completely risk-free.

---

### Who controls the funds?

Funds are managed through a structured system:

- User deposits are routed via smart contracts  
- Operational controls govern execution  
- Policies restrict unauthorized movement  

---

### What happens if something goes wrong?

Risks exist, including:

- Smart contract issues  
- Counterparty failure  
- Market-related losses  

Users should understand all risks before participating.

---

## Technical

### Do I need to sign transactions?

Yes.

- Deposits require wallet signatures  
- Withdrawals do not require user signatures  

---

### Are transactions on-chain?

Deposits and certain interactions are on-chain.

Other processes may involve off-chain systems
for execution and settlement.

---

## Final Notes

- RondSync does not guarantee returns  
- Users are responsible for their decisions  
- Always verify information before interacting  

For more details, refer to the Risk Disclosure and Security sections.
