## What is RondoSync?

RondoSync is not a single strategy.

It is a system that:

- Aggregates multiple return-seeking strategies  
- Routes capital dynamically  
- Applies controlled execution layers  

---

## Core Concept

RondoSync is built on three principles:

### 1. Structured Capital Allocation
Capital is organized into Vaults with defined parameters.

### 2. Controlled Execution
All capital flows through policy-controlled infrastructure.

### 3. Transparent Profit distribution
**Profit** and loss are calculated and reflected through a structured system (in the app, **Rewards** may label the same idea).
