## Overview

Users can redeem their Vault positions based on each Vault’s conditions.

- Redemption converts Vault shares into USD balance  
- Conditions vary depending on the Vault  
- Early redemption may include penalties  

---

## Redemption Flow

```mermaid
flowchart LR
    A[User Request] --> B[Redemption Validation]
    B --> C[Penalty Calculation]
    C --> D[Settlement Process]
    D --> E[USD Balance]
```

---

## Redemption Process

### Step 1 — Request

Users initiate redemption from their portfolio.

- Select Vault position  
- Specify amount (partial or full)  
- Submit request  

---

### Step 2 — Validation

The system verifies eligibility.

- Lock-up conditions  
- Vault-specific rules  
- Current position status  

---

### Step 3 — Penalty Calculation

If applicable, penalties are calculated.

- Early redemption fees  
- Strategy-related adjustments  
- Liquidity-related costs  

---

### Step 4 — Settlement

The final amount is settled and allocated.

- Net value is calculated  
- Shares are burned or adjusted  
- Resulting amount is added to USD balance  

---

## Redemption Types

### Full Redemption

- Exit entire Vault position  
- All shares are converted  

---

### Partial Redemption

- Exit a portion of the position  
- Remaining shares continue to participate  

---

## Lock-Up Conditions

Some Vaults may enforce lock-up periods.

- Funds cannot be redeemed during lock-up  
- Early exit may be restricted or penalized  

---

## Penalties

Penalties may apply under certain conditions.

- Early exit fees  
- Strategy unwind costs  
- Liquidity impact adjustments  

Penalty structures vary by Vault.

---

## Settlement Timing

Redemption may not be instantaneous.

- Depends on Vault structure  
- Strategy execution timing  
- Liquidity availability  

---

## Important Considerations

- Redemption conditions vary per Vault  
- Early exit may reduce returns  
- Final value depends on market conditions  
- Partial redemption affects remaining exposure  

---

## Relationship to Withdrawal

Redemption and withdrawal are separate processes:

- Redemption → converts Vault position into USD balance  
- Withdrawal → transfers USD balance to user wallet  

---

## Summary

Redemption enables users to:

- Exit Vault positions  
- Manage capital allocation  
- Control exposure to strategies  

All redemptions are subject to Vault-specific rules
and market conditions.
